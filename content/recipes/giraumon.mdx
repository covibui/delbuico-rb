---
slug: giraumon
title: Soupe De Giraumon Le Recif
author: Miche
group: soups
prep_time:
  time: 10
  unit: minute
cook_time:
  time: 45
  unit: minute
servings:
  minimum: 4
ingredients:
  - item: 1/2 pound salt pork, cut into 1-inch pieces
  - item: 1 1/2 pounds beef soup meat
  - item: 6 cups water
  - item: 2 pounds calabaza, peeled and cut into pieces
  - item: 2 scallions, including the green tops, chopped
  - item: 1 shallot, minced
  - item: 2 chives, minced
  - item: 1 scotch bonnet-type chile, pricked with a fork
  - item: 1/2 cup cooked spaghetti, cut into pieces
directions:
  - step: >-

      Place the salt pork in a large saucepan and cook it over medium heat until it renders its fat and begins to brown. Add the soup meat and brown it in the rendered fat. Add the water and bring the contents of the pot to a boil. Lower the heat and cook for about 45 minutes, or until the meat is tender.

  - step: >-

      Add the calabaza and the remaining ingredients except the pasta and cook until they have completely disintegrated. Check the seasonings and add the spaghetti. Cook until the spaghetti is heated through, stirring to make sure that the soup is well mixed. Serve hot in bowls.
