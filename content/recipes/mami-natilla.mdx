  minimum: 4
ingredients:
  - item: 32 oz of whole milk
  - item: 1 cup of sugar or condensed milk
  - item: 6 Tbs cornstarch
  - item: 1 tsp vanilla
  - item: 6 Yolks
  - item: 1 cinnamon stick
post_recipe_notes: >-

  *Heat milk with cinnamon.Mix yolks until frothy and light in color.Then add condensed milk (or sugar) and mix well.Temper hot milk into the yolk mixture.Place in a double boiler and keep stirring clock wise until thick.Add vanilla at the end.In a serving bowl place nilla wafers at the bottom of a bowl.Top with natilla when thick.*
