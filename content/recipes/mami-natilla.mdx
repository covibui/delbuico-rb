title: Abuelita Mami's Natilla
author: Alexis
group: desserts
prep_time:
  time: 30
  unit: minute
cook_time:
  time: 30
  unit: minute
servings:
  minimum: 4
ingredients:
  - item: 32 oz of whole milk
  - item: 1 cup of sugar or condensed milk
  - item: 6 Tbs cornstarch
  - item: 1 tsp vanilla
  - item: 6 Yolks
  - item: 1 cinnamon stick
directions:
  - step: >-

      Heat milk with cinnamon.
  - step: >-

      Mix yolks until frothy and light in color.
  - step: >-

      Then add condensed milk (or sugar) and mix well.
  - step: >-

      Temper hot milk into the yolk mixture.
  - step: >-

      Place in a double boiler and keep stirring clock wise until thick.
  - step: >-

      Add vanilla at the end.In a serving bowl place nilla wafers at the bottom of a bowl.Top with natilla when thick.
