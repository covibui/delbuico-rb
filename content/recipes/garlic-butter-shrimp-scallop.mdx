slug: garlic-butter-shrimp-scallop
title: Garlic Butter Shrimp n Scallops
author: Miche
group: shellfish-fish
prep_time:
  time: 20
  unit: minute
cook_time:
  time: 20
  unit: minute
servings:
  minimum: 4
ingredients:
  - item: 4 tbsp unsalted butter
  - item: 4 large garlic cloves, chopped
  - item: 1/2-1 cup Pinot Grigio
  - item: 2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  - item: 1/2 pound or 454g large shrimp, peeled
  - item: 10 large scallops
  - item: Salt and fresh ground black pepper to taste
  - item: 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper flakes or to taste (optional)
  - item: 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  - item: 4 tbsp Parmesan cheese, grated
directions:
  - step: >-

      Preheat your oven to 350 F.
  - step: >-

      Place skillet on grates and preheat for about 5 minutes. Heat butter and add garlic. Sauté until fragrant about 1-2 minutes. Add wine, lemon juice, shrimp, scallops, salt, pepper, red pepper flakes, and parsley. Mix it all up and add parmesan.
  - step: >-

      Bring to a simmer or until the liquid reduces by about half and the shrimp/scallops are fully cooked. About 30 minutes.
  - step: >-

      Serve with bread for dipping or over pasta/rice. Garnish with parsley and lemon slices (optional).
---
