title: Rice with Lima Beans
author: Miche
group: grains
prep_time:
  time: 10
  unit: minute
cook_time:
  time: 25
  unit: minute
servings: 4
ingredients:
  - item: 1 cup of rice (white or brown)
    substitute: 1 cup of water (for white rice) or 1 3/4 cup of water (for brown rice)
  - item: "1/2 cup of coconut milk "
    substitute: (may substitute for water or broth)
  - item: 1/4 tsp salt
    substitute: ""
  - item: 1/2 cup of frozen lima beans
  - item: "4 tablespoons of  unseasoned tomato sauce "
    substitute: (may use 1 large fresh tomato)
  - item: 2 to 3 tbsp of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  - item: Fresh seasoning mix (garlic, green onion, and parsley)
directions:
  - step: >-

      1. Heat up a saucepan, add oil, a pinch of your fresh seasoning mix


      2. Add lima beans


      3. Sprinkle salt to sizzling lima beans.


      4. Add tomato sauce (or fresh tomato)


      5. Add [coconut milk](http://sweetcoconuts.blogspot.com/2011/02/making-coconut-milk.html) (may substitute for water or broth)


      6. Add water


      7. Bring to a boil


      8. Stir in rice


      9. Once liquid has evaporated, reduce heat, cover pan, and simmer for 20 minutes.
