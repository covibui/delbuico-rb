---
slug: rice-lima-beans
title: Rice with Lima Beans
author: Miche
group: grains
prep_time:
  time: 10
  unit: minute
cook_time:
  time: 25
  unit: minute
servings:
  minimum: 4
ingredients:
  - item: 1 cup of rice (white or brown)
    substitute: 1 cup of water (for white rice) or 1 3/4 cup of water (for brown rice)
  - item: "1/2 cup of coconut milk "
    substitute: may substitute for water or broth
  - item: 1/4 tsp salt
    substitute: ""
  - item: 1/2 cup of frozen lima beans
  - item: "4 tablespoons of  unseasoned tomato sauce "
    substitute: (may use 1 large fresh tomato)
  - item: 2 to 3 tbsp of Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  - item: Fresh seasoning mix (garlic, green onion, and parsley)
directions:
  - step: >-

      Heat up a saucepan, add oil, a pinch of your fresh seasoning mix
  - step: >-

      Add lima beans
  - step: >-

      Sprinkle salt to sizzling lima beans.
  - step: >-

      Add tomato sauce (or fresh tomato)
  - step: >-

      Add [coconut milk](http://sweetcoconuts.blogspot.com/2011/02/making-coconut-milk.html) (may substitute for water or broth)
  - step: >-

      Add water
  - step: >-

      Bring to a boil
  - step: >-

      Stir in rice
  - step: >-

      Once liquid has evaporated, reduce heat, cover pan, and simmer for 20 minutes.
