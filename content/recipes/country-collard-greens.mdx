title: Melba’s Country Collard Greens
author: Miche
group: vegetables
prep_time:
  time: 20
  unit: minute
cook_time:
  time: 1
  unit: hour
servings: 8
ingredients:
  - item: 3 cups chicken stock
  - item: 1 cup water
  - item: 2 lbs fresh collard greens
  - item: ½ lb smoked turkey wings
  - item: 2 yellow onions, chopped
  - item: 4 cloves garlic, minced
  - item: ½ tsp light brown sugar
  - item: 2 tbsp sea salt
  - item: 1 tbsp freshly ground black pepper
  - item: 1 tbsp red pepper flakes
  - item: 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  - item: 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
  - item: 1 tbsp hot sauce of your choice
directions:
  - step: >-
      
      1. Bring the chicken stock and water to a boil in a large heavy pot over medium heat.


      2. Wash the greens thoroughly in cold running water and cut off the tough stem ends. Lay the greens on top of one another and cut them into ½ inch wide slices.


      3. Put them in the boiling water along with all the remaining ingredients. Stir reduce the heat to low, and cover.


      4. Cook on low for 45 to 60 minutes, stirring every 15 minutes, until the greens are tender.
