---
slug: fried-turkey
title: Deep Fried Turkey
author: Miche
group: poultry
prep_time:
  time: 1
  unit: day
cook_time:
  time: 45
  unit: minute
servings: 8
ingredients:
  - item: ¼ c chili powder
  - item: ¼ c paprika
  - item: ¼ c ground black pepper
  - item: 2 tbls creole salt
  - item: 1 ½ tbls onion powder
  - item: 2 tbls garlic
  - item: 1 ½ tbls cayenne powder
  - item: 2tsp thyme
  - item: 1Tbl oregano
  - item: 1 stick of butter
  - item: 8 tbs veggie oil
  - item: 1 bay leaf
  - item: 2 cloves garlic
  - item: cajun seasoning
  - item: ¾ c hot water
  - item: 1tbs Worcestershire sauce
  - item: 1 habanero
directions:
  - step: >-
      
      Mix all rub ingredients together and apply ample quantities to the outside of the turkey. Let meat marinate for 8-24 hours.
  - step: >-
      
      Combine injection recipe ingredients including 1/4 to 1/2 cup of the rub; boil and reduce to 1 cup. Cool and strain mixture then inject into all areas of the turkey.
  - step: |-
      
      Fry turkey according to the weight of bird and fryer instructions.
  - step: >-
      
      Maintain the temperature of the oil at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C), and cook the turkey for 3 1/2 minutes per pound, about 45 minutes. Carefully remove the basket from oil, and drain turkey. Insert a meat thermometer into the thickest part of the thigh; the internal temperature must be 180 degrees F (80 degrees C).
