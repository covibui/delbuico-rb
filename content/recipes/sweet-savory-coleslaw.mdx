---
slug: sweet-savory-coleslaw
title: Sweet 'n' Savory Coleslaw
author: Miche
group: salads-dressings
prep_time:
  time: 10
  unit: minute
cook_time:
  time: 4
  unit: hour
servings:
  minimum: 6
ingredients:
  - item: ¾ cup mayonnaise, or to taste
  - item: 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
  - item: ¼ teaspoon salt
  - item: ¼ cup sugar
  - item: 3 cups peeled and shredded carrots
  - item: 1 (3-pound) head of green cabbage, shredded
  - item: ¼ cup thinly sliced scallions, green part only
  - item: ¼ teaspoon celery seed
directions:
  - step: >-

      1. In a medium bowl, whisk together the mayonnaise, relish, salt, and sugar.


      2. Add all of the remaining ingredients and mix to combine well.


      3. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours and mix again just before serving.
post_recipe_notes: >-

  Mayo-less Coleslaw - Some people prefer their coleslaw with little or even no mayo. Maybe that 'show your mama made yours. It's more a German-style dish, almost like a pickled relish, and that's good, too. I just happen to like mine with lots of mayo, the way my own mother made it.
