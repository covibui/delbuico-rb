---
slug: white-rice
title: White Rice
author: Miche
group: grains
prep_time:
  time: 5
  unit: minute
cook_time:
  time: 20
  unit: minute
servings:
  minimum: 3
ingredients:
  - item: 1 cup of white rice
  - item: 1 1/2 cup of water
  - item: 1/4 tsp salt
  - item: 2 tbsp of Extra Virgin Olive oil
directions:
  - step: >-

      In a saucepan add water, oil, salt.
  - step: >-

      Bring to a boil
  - step: >-

      Stir in white rice
  - step: >-

      When liquid has evaporated, reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
---
