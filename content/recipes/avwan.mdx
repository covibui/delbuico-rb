author: Miche
group: breakfast
prep_time:
  time: 5
  unit: minute
cook_time:
  time: 25
  unit: minute
servings:
  minimum: 3
materials:
  - item: Medium sized Saucepan
ingredients:
  - item: 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
  - item: 4 cups water
  - item: "1/2 cup sugar "
    substitute: and sugar to taste
  - item: 1/4 teaspoon salt
  - item: 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
  - item: 1 star anise
  - item: 2 cinnamon sticks
  - item: 1 can (12oz) evaporate milk
  - item: 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - item: 1 tablespoon butter
directions:
  - step: >-

      In a medium-sized saucepan on medium to high heat add 4 cups of water, star anise, and cinnamon sticks, and bring to a boil
  - step: >-

      Once it starts boiling add milk, sugar, salt, nutmeg, and oats and mix well.
  - step: >-

      Remove the star anise lower the flame and let cook for 15 minutes.
  - step: >-

      Add butter, and vanilla extract and let simmer for another 10 minutes. Serve will hot, enjoy
